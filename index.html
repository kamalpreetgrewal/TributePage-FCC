Baba Banda Singh Bahadur Ji

The Sikh Military Commander

Here's a timeline of Baba Banda Singh Ji's life:

  • 1670- Born in Jammu region of Jammu & Kashmir. He was named Lachman Dev.
  • 1685- Became an ascetic recluse and was given the name Madho Das.
  • Established a monastery in Nanded on the bank of river Godavari.
  • 1708- Visited by, and became disciple of Guru Gobind Singh Ji and was named Banda Bahadur.
  • 1709- Sacked Mughal provincial capital, Samana.
  • Abolished zamindari system and granted proper rights to tillers of the land.
  • 1716- Captured by Mughals and tortured to death.
